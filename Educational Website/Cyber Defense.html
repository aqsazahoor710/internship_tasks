The Zero-Trust Era

In a hyper-connected landscape, the traditional "perimeter" defense is obsolete. Sapphire’s Cyber Defense curriculum is built on the Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA)—a security model that requires strict identity verification for every person and device trying to access resources on a private network, regardless of whether they are sitting inside or outside of the network perimeter.

Students engage in rigorous simulations of modern threat vectors, including ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) and supply-chain vulnerabilities, preparing for a battlefield where the enemy is often invisible and automated.
